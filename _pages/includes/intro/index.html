    I am a post-doctoral researcher with School of Future Technology (未来技术学院) at South China University of Technology (华南理工大学), working with Prof. Mingkui Tan (谭明奎) and Prof. Cen Chen (陈岑). I received my bachelor and PHD degree from South China University of Technology in 2018 and 2024, respectively. My research interests include efficient architecture design and out-of-distribution generalization. I published papers in top venues, including ICML, ICLR, CVPR, AAAI, IEEE TCSVT and Neural Networks. As of now, my research has been cited more than 945 times, as reported by Google Scholar. I served as the reviewer for several top-tier machine learning/computer vision conferences, including ICLR, ICML, NeurIPS, CVPR, ICCV, ECCV and AAAI.
